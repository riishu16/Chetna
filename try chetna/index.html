About C.H.E.T.N.A

C.H.E.T.N.A empowers students and professionals to reflect on their mental wellbeing through structured self-assessments. At CHETNA, we believe in the power of awareness, compassion, and action. Our mission is to create meaningful change by bringing people together, inspiring new ideas, and driving impact where it matters most. Guided by our core values of empathy, transparency, and innovation, CHETNA serves as a platform for growth—whether it’s empowering communities, nurturing creativity, or advancing knowledge. We are more than just a name—we are a movement towards progress, driven by the belief that small steps can spark big transformations.
